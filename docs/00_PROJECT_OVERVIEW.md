# SANA Digital Platform - Project Overview

## Introduction

SANA Digital Platform is a healthcare technology ecosystem designed to digitize and manage the operations of SANA Hijama & Integrative Medicine Center.

The platform aims to provide an integrated environment connecting patients, practitioners, administrators, and healthcare services through modern digital solutions.

## Project Goals

- Digitize clinic operations.
- Improve patient experience.
- Enable online appointments and communication.
- Provide secure medical records management.
- Support analytics and intelligent healthcare decisions.

## Scope

The platform includes:

- Patient management.
- Appointment scheduling.
- Electronic medical records.
- Doctor management.
- Billing and reports.
- Mobile applications.
- AI-assisted healthcare features.

## Future Vision

SANA Digital Platform is designed as a scalable healthcare SaaS solution that can support multiple clinics and healthcare providers.
