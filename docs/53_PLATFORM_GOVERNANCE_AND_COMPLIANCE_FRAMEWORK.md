# Platform Governance and Compliance Framework

## 1. Overview

The Sana Digital Platform Governance Framework establishes the policies, standards, responsibilities, and control mechanisms required to ensure secure, reliable, and compliant healthcare platform operations.

The governance framework supports:

- Secure healthcare operations
- Regulatory alignment
- Data protection
- Operational accountability
- Continuous improvement

---

## 2. Governance Objectives

The main objectives are:

- Establish clear platform ownership
- Define operational responsibilities
- Maintain healthcare compliance standards
- Protect patient information
- Manage technology risks
- Improve platform maturity

---

## 3. Governance Structure

The governance model consists of several levels:

### 3.1 Executive Governance

Responsibilities:

- Strategic planning
- Business objectives alignment
- Investment decisions
- Risk acceptance
- Platform growth direction

---

### 3.2 Technical Governance

Responsibilities:

- Architecture standards
- Technology selection
- Development guidelines
- Infrastructure policies
- System quality management

---

### 3.3 Security Governance

Responsibilities:

- Security policies
- Identity management
- Access control
- Threat monitoring
- Security assessments

---

### 3.4 Data Governance

Responsibilities:

- Data ownership
- Data quality management
- Data classification
- Data lifecycle control
- Privacy protection

---

## 4. Healthcare Compliance Management

The platform follows healthcare technology compliance principles including:

- Patient data protection
- Secure medical record management
- Confidentiality controls
- Audit capabilities
- Authorized access management

---

## 5. Data Governance Framework

Data governance includes:

- Data classification standards
- Data ownership definition
- Data validation processes
- Data retention policies
- Data access rules
- Data quality monitoring

---

## 6. Privacy Framework

Patient privacy is protected through:

- Identity protection mechanisms
- Role-based access control
- Secure authentication
- Data encryption
- Privacy-focused system design

---

## 7. Policy Management

The platform maintains policies covering:

- Information security
- User management
- Medical data handling
- System usage
- Backup procedures
- Incident response

---

## 8. Audit and Accountability

Audit capabilities include:

- User activity tracking
- Administrative action logs
- Security event monitoring
- Compliance reports
- Investigation records

---

## 9. Risk Management Framework

Risk management process:

1. Risk identification
2. Risk assessment
3. Risk prioritization
4. Risk mitigation
5. Risk monitoring
6. Continuous improvement

---

## 10. Compliance Monitoring

Continuous monitoring activities include:

- Security reviews
- Access reviews
- Policy validation
- System audits
- Operational assessments

---

## 11. Governance Improvement

Governance maturity is improved through:

- Regular framework reviews
- Technology updates
- Security enhancements
- Process optimization
- Stakeholder feedback

---

## 12. Conclusion

The Sana Digital Platform Governance Framework provides a structured approach for managing healthcare technology operations while ensuring security, compliance, transparency, and sustainable growth.
