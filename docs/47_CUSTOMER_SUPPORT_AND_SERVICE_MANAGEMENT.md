# Sana Digital Platform - Customer Support and Service Management

## 1. Overview

This document defines the customer support and service management framework for the Sana Digital Platform to ensure effective assistance, issue resolution, and customer satisfaction.

## 2. Support Objectives

- Provide reliable customer assistance.
- Maintain service availability.
- Resolve issues efficiently.
- Improve customer experience.

## 3. Support Levels

### Level 1 Support

Includes:

- User assistance.
- Basic troubleshooting.
- Account support.
- Common issue resolution.

### Level 2 Support

Includes:

- Technical investigation.
- Configuration issues.
- Integration support.

### Level 3 Support

Includes:

- Advanced engineering support.
- Software defects.
- Architecture-level problems.

## 4. Support Channels

Supported channels:

- Help desk.
- Email support.
- Knowledge base.
- Technical communication channels.

## 5. Ticket Management

Each request should include:

- Issue description.
- Priority level.
- Customer information.
- Resolution status.
- Support history.

## 6. Service Level Management

Includes:

- Response time targets.
- Resolution commitments.
- Escalation procedures.
- Service quality monitoring.

## 7. Knowledge Management

Support resources:

- User guides.
- Troubleshooting articles.
- Frequently asked questions.
- Technical documentation.

## 8. Customer Feedback

Feedback is used for:

- Service improvements.
- Product enhancements.
- Support quality evaluation.

## 9. Support Analytics

Metrics include:

- Ticket volume.
- Resolution time.
- Customer satisfaction.
- Common issue analysis.

## 10. Future Enhancements

- AI-powered support assistant.
- Automated ticket classification.
- Predictive issue detection.
