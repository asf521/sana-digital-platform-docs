# Sana Digital Platform - Subscription and SaaS Business Model

## 1. Overview

This document defines the subscription and SaaS business model strategy for the Sana Digital Platform to support scalable healthcare services and sustainable platform growth.

## 2. Business Model Objectives

- Provide flexible healthcare software services.
- Support multiple clinic customers.
- Enable recurring revenue models.
- Simplify service expansion.

## 3. SaaS Model Structure

The platform supports:

- Multi-tenant healthcare services.
- Cloud-based access.
- Subscription-based features.
- Continuous platform updates.

## 4. Subscription Plans

Possible plans include:

- Starter plan for small clinics.
- Professional plan for growing healthcare providers.
- Enterprise plan for large organizations.

## 5. Feature Management

Subscription features may include:

- User limits.
- Appointment management.
- Medical records.
- Analytics and reporting.
- AI healthcare capabilities.

## 6. Tenant Billing Management

Includes:

- Subscription tracking.
- Invoice management.
- Payment status monitoring.
- Plan upgrades and downgrades.

## 7. SaaS Operations

Operational requirements:

- Tenant provisioning.
- Service monitoring.
- Usage analytics.
- Customer support processes.

## 8. Scalability Considerations

The model supports:

- Growing customer base.
- Additional healthcare modules.
- Regional expansion.
- Enterprise deployments.

## 9. Customer Success Management

Includes:

- Customer onboarding.
- Usage analysis.
- Training support.
- Feedback collection.

## 10. Future Enhancements

- Automated subscription management.
- AI-driven business insights.
- Marketplace for healthcare integrations.
