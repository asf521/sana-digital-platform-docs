# Platform Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Framework

## 1. Overview

The Sana Digital Platform Customer Relationship Management Framework defines the strategy, processes, and digital tools required to manage relationships between the healthcare organization, patients, providers, and partners.

The CRM framework improves:

- Patient engagement
- Communication quality
- Service personalization
- Retention management
- Business growth

---

## 2. CRM Objectives

The main objectives include:

- Building long-term patient relationships
- Improving patient satisfaction
- Managing communication channels
- Supporting marketing activities
- Increasing service loyalty

---

## 3. CRM Architecture Components

The CRM architecture includes:

- Patient profiles
- Communication management
- Interaction history
- Feedback management
- Campaign management
- Relationship analytics

---

## 4. Patient Relationship Management

Patient relationship capabilities include:

- Complete patient profiles
- Medical service history
- Communication preferences
- Appointment history
- Treatment engagement tracking
