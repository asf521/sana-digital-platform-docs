# Sana Digital Platform - Privacy and Healthcare Data Governance

## 1. Overview

This document defines the privacy and healthcare data governance framework for the Sana Digital Platform to ensure responsible handling, protection, and management of sensitive health information.

## 2. Governance Objectives

- Protect patient privacy.
- Ensure responsible data usage.
- Maintain data accuracy and integrity.
- Support healthcare compliance requirements.

## 3. Data Classification

Data categories include:

- Patient personal information.
- Medical records.
- Treatment information.
- Financial information.
- System operational data.

## 4. Privacy Principles

The platform follows:

- Data minimization.
- Purpose limitation.
- Secure processing.
- Controlled access.
- Transparency of data usage.

## 5. Access Governance

Controls include:

- Role-based access control.
- Permission management.
- Access reviews.
- Activity auditing.

## 6. Healthcare Data Protection

Protection measures:

- Encryption of sensitive information.
- Secure medical record storage.
- Controlled document access.
- Data backup protection.

## 7. Data Lifecycle Management

Lifecycle stages:

1. Data collection.
2. Data processing.
3. Data storage.
4. Data usage.
5. Data retention.
6. Secure disposal.

## 8. Consent Management

The platform should support:

- Patient consent records.
- Consent updates.
- Data usage preferences.
- Audit history.

## 9. Compliance Management

Includes:

- Privacy policy enforcement.
- Regulatory requirement tracking.
- Security reviews.
- Governance documentation.

## 10. Continuous Improvement

Future enhancements:

- Automated privacy monitoring.
- AI-based compliance analysis.
- Advanced data governance dashboards.
