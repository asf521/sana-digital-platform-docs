# SANA Digital Platform - Executive Summary

## Purpose

SANA Digital Platform is a strategic digital transformation initiative for SANA Hijama & Integrative Medicine Center.

The platform provides a complete healthcare management ecosystem that improves operational efficiency, patient engagement, and clinical service quality.

## Key Objectives

- Build a unified digital healthcare environment.
- Automate clinic workflows.
- Improve accessibility through web and mobile applications.
- Provide secure healthcare data management.
- Enable future expansion into a SaaS healthcare platform.

## Target Users

### Patients

- Online booking.
- Medical history access.
- Treatment follow-up.
- Notifications and communication.

### Healthcare Providers

- Patient records.
- Treatment management.
- Clinical documentation.
- Reports and analytics.

### Administrators

- User management.
- Financial monitoring.
- System configuration.
- Business intelligence.

## Expected Impact

The platform will reduce manual processes, enhance patient experience, and create a scalable foundation for modern integrative healthcare services.
