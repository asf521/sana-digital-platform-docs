# Platform Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan

## 1. Overview

The Sana Digital Platform Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan defines the strategies, procedures, and technical controls required to maintain healthcare service availability during unexpected incidents, failures, or disruptive events.

The plan ensures:

- Continuous healthcare operations
- Data protection
- Rapid service restoration
- Operational resilience
- Patient service continuity

---

## 2. Business Continuity Objectives

The main objectives include:

- Minimizing service interruptions
- Protecting critical healthcare information
- Maintaining essential platform functions
- Reducing operational risks
- Ensuring rapid recovery

---

## 3. Critical Platform Services

Critical services include:

- Patient management system
- Appointment management
- Medical records
- User authentication
- Payment services
- Notification systems
- Reporting services

---

## 4. Risk Assessment Framework

Potential risks include:

- Infrastructure failures
- Database failures
- Cybersecurity incidents
- Network interruptions
- Software defects
- Human errors
- Natural disasters

---

## 5. Disaster Recovery Architecture

The recovery architecture includes:

- Backup systems
- Recovery environments
- Data replication
- System restoration procedures
- Emergency access mechanisms

---

## 6. Backup Strategy

Backup management includes:

- Automated database backups
- Application configuration backups
- Secure backup storage
- Backup verification
- Recovery testing

---

## 7. Recovery Objectives

The platform defines:

### Recovery Time Objective (RTO)

The maximum acceptable time required to restore services after disruption.

### Recovery Point Objective (RPO)

The maximum acceptable amount of data loss measured in time.

---

## 8. Incident Recovery Process

Recovery workflow:

1. Incident detection
2. Impact assessment
3. Recovery activation
4. Service restoration
5. Data validation
6. Business resumption
7. Post-incident analysis

---

## 9. High Availability Strategy

Availability is improved through:

- Redundant infrastructure
- Load balancing
- Service monitoring
- Automated recovery
- Preventive maintenance

---

## 10. Communication Plan

During incidents, communication includes:

- Internal notifications
- Technical team coordination
- Business stakeholder updates
- Service status reporting

---

## 11. Disaster Recovery Testing

Testing activities include:

- Backup restoration tests
- Recovery simulations
- System failover testing
- Process evaluation

---

## 12. Continuous Improvement

The recovery framework improves through:

- Incident reviews
- Risk reassessment
- Technology updates
- Process optimization

---

## 13. Conclusion

The Sana Digital Platform Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan ensures reliable healthcare service delivery by preparing the platform to withstand disruptions and recover efficiently.
