# Sana Digital Platform - Artificial Intelligence Governance and Ethics

## 1. Overview

This document defines the AI governance and ethics framework for the Sana Digital Platform to ensure responsible, safe, and transparent use of artificial intelligence technologies.

## 2. AI Governance Objectives

- Ensure safe AI usage in healthcare environments.
- Protect patient privacy.
- Maintain transparency and accountability.
- Reduce AI-related risks.

## 3. AI Usage Principles

The platform follows:

- Human oversight.
- Responsible automation.
- Explainable AI decisions.
- Secure AI data processing.

## 4. AI Data Governance

Controls include:

- Data quality management.
- Training data protection.
- Data privacy enforcement.
- Access restrictions.

## 5. AI Model Management

Includes:

- Model evaluation.
- Performance monitoring.
- Version control.
- Validation before deployment.

## 6. Healthcare AI Safety

Requirements:

- Clinical decision support limitations.
- Professional review of AI recommendations.
- Avoiding unsupported medical conclusions.
- Continuous accuracy evaluation.

## 7. AI Security

Security measures:

- Protection of AI models.
- Secure AI APIs.
- Access control.
- Monitoring abnormal usage.

## 8. AI Ethics Framework

Principles:

- Fairness.
- Transparency.
- Privacy.
- Accountability.
- Safety.

## 9. AI Compliance Management

Includes:

- Policy documentation.
- Risk assessment.
- Audit records.
- Governance reviews.

## 10. Future Enhancements

- Advanced AI monitoring.
- Automated governance workflows.
- AI risk scoring.
- Intelligent compliance analysis.
