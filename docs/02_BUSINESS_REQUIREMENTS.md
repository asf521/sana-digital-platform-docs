# SANA Digital Platform - Business Requirements

## Overview

This document defines the business requirements for SANA Digital Platform.

## Business Goals

- Improve clinic operational efficiency.
- Provide better patient services.
- Reduce manual administrative tasks.
- Enable data-driven healthcare decisions.

## Functional Requirements

### Patient Management

- Patient registration and profiles.
- Medical history management.
- Treatment records.
- Appointment tracking.

### Appointment System

- Online booking.
- Schedule management.
- Reminders and notifications.

### Clinic Operations

- Doctor management.
- Treatment workflow management.
- Billing and reporting.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- High security standards.
- Data privacy protection.
- Scalability.
- High availability.
- User-friendly interface.

## User Roles

- Patient.
- Doctor.
- Receptionist.
- Administrator.
- System Manager.
