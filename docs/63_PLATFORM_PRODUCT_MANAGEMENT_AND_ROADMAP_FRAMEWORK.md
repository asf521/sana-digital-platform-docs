# Platform Product Management and Roadmap Framework

## 1. Overview

The Sana Digital Platform Product Management Framework defines the strategy, processes, and methodologies used to manage platform evolution, prioritize features, and deliver continuous healthcare technology improvements.

The framework ensures alignment between:

- Business objectives
- Healthcare requirements
- User expectations
- Technical capabilities

---

## 2. Product Management Objectives

The main objectives include:

- Defining product vision
- Managing feature priorities
- Supporting continuous innovation
- Improving user value
- Ensuring sustainable growth

---

## 3. Product Vision

The Sana Digital Platform aims to become a comprehensive healthcare technology ecosystem that connects:

- Patients
- Healthcare providers
- Clinics
- Medical services
- Digital health solutions

---

## 4. Product Strategy

Product strategy includes:

- Market analysis
- User needs assessment
- Feature planning
- Technology evaluation
- Growth opportunities
