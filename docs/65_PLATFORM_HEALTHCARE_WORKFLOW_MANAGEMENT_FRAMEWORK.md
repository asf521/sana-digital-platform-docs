# Platform Healthcare Workflow Management Framework

## 1. Overview

The Sana Digital Platform Healthcare Workflow Management Framework defines the processes, automation methods, and operational structures required to manage healthcare activities efficiently across the platform.

The framework ensures:

- Consistent healthcare workflows
- Improved provider efficiency
- Better patient experience
- Reduced operational complexity
- Standardized clinical processes

---

## 2. Workflow Management Objectives

The main objectives include:

- Automating healthcare operations
- Improving service delivery
- Reducing manual tasks
- Supporting clinical decision-making
- Enhancing operational visibility

---

## 3. Core Healthcare Workflows

The platform manages:

- Patient registration
- Appointment scheduling
- Patient consultation
- Treatment management
- Follow-up procedures
- Billing workflows
- Reporting processes
