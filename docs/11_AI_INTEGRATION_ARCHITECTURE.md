# 11 - AI Integration Architecture
# Sana Digital Platform

## 1. AI Vision

The Sana Digital Platform AI layer introduces intelligent healthcare capabilities to improve patient experience, support medical teams, and optimize clinic operations.

The AI architecture is designed as an independent layer that can evolve with future healthcare technologies.
